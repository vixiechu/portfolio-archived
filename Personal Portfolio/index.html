About

Gamer, Animal Lover & Aspiring Front End Developer

I first discovered my interest in Web Development when I was ill and stuck at home a few years back. I needed something to occupy my mind while I recovered; so, I self-taught some HTML5! This choice ultimately lead me to embark on the BSc Web Development course at Manchester Metropolitan University, of which I am in my final year. Making the decision to leave a successful career in finance, follow my heart and study something I found challenging and enjoyable was very nerve-wracking!

Once University began, I was incredibly eager to start expanding my knowledge and I quickly realised the nerves I initially felt stemmed from wanting to succeed in a new and exciting area; one that would consistently challenge me and allow me to experience the digital world from a different, more professional perspective. I purchased the Jon Duckett books in my first year to help me further understand the languages I was learning, and the rest, as they say is history!

Although currently working in social media, I aspire to obtain a career in Front End Development or UXD. I find both of these topics not only interesting, but incredibly insightful. I enjoy UXD, as there is so much insight and knowledge to be gained from the simplest of things; such as user habits, journeys and application efficiency. Relating to Front End web development, I enjoy utilising the programming languages that I have learnt so far, and applying them to one another successfully, and I'm certain that I would enjoy a career where I could put these skills into practice daily.
